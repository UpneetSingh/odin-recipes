Boiled Eggs

Description

Create perfect boiled eggs with this simple recipe. Start by placing eggs in a single layer at the bottom of a saucepan. Cover them with water, ensuring they are fully submerged. Bring the water to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Allow the eggs to simmer for 9-12 minutes, depending on your desired donenessâ€”less time for soft-boiled and more for hard-boiled. Once cooked, transfer the eggs to an ice water bath to cool and make peeling easier. Peel, sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper, and relish these versatile, protein-packed treats as a snack or in various dishes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
  2. Bring water to a gentle boil and then reduce heat to simmer.
  3. Allow eggs to simmer for 9-12 minutes.
  4. Transfer eggs to an ice water bath to cool quickly.
  5. Peel, season with salt and pepper, and enjoy as a snack or in dishes
