Pizza

Description

Prepare a delicious homemade pizza with this simple recipe. Start by preheating your oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface, creating the desired thickness. Spread a layer of rich tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously, and add your favorite toppings—pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives work well. Drizzle with olive oil for extra flavor.Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Garnish with fresh basil and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan. Slice, serve, and enjoy your homemade pizza masterpiece!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives)
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to the desired thickness.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Generously sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives.
  6. Drizzle the pizza with olive oil for extra flavor.
  7. Transfer the prepared pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Garnish the finished pizza with fresh basil and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan.
  10. Slice, serve, and enjoy your homemade pizza masterpiece!
